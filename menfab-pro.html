About MENFAB

12 years of passion · Crafted in Bangladesh · Worn worldwide

MENFAB
2012
Founded
Our Philosophy

Dressed for Every Chapter

At MENFAB, we believe clothing is more than fabric — it is an expression of character. Founded in Chattogram in 2012, we set out with one goal: to create premium men's fashion that reflects the ambition and elegance of the modern Bangladeshi man.

Every piece is thoughtfully designed with premium materials — cotton, linen, wool — tailored for the man who moves through the world with intention, confidence, and ease.
